About this free image compressor

Shrink and resize JPG, PNG and WebP images right in your browser. Because compression happens on your device, your photos are never uploaded — ideal for private images and slow connections.

How to use

  1. Drop in or select an image.
  2. Adjust the quality level or target size.
  3. Download the smaller file.

Why use it

Large images slow down websites and fill up storage. Compressing before you upload speeds up pages and saves bandwidth, and doing it locally keeps private photos private.

FAQ

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No — compression runs entirely in your browser; files never leave your device.

Which formats are supported?

Common web formats including JPG, PNG and WebP.

Will quality drop noticeably?

You control the quality/size trade-off; modest compression is usually visually lossless.
